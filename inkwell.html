Paperwork

Papel is modeled on the best transaction coordinators. Built for speed and accuracy, it handles the full file from purchase agreement to closing.

Purchase agreements

The PA-1 prepared from your terms, with the price, dates, and contingencies where they belong.

Disclosures

Seller's disclosures assembled, filled from the record, and routed to the right signers.

Addenda and counters

FHA, inspection, radon, counter-offers. Prepared from your email, at your direction, when the deal shifts.

Deadline tracking

Inspection, financing, and closing dates watched through the whole deal. Reminders arrive with the date in the subject line.

Signature routing

Documents go out for e-signature in the right order, to the right people. You see who signed and who hasn't.

Status updates

Review

Built for Minnesota

The forms, known cold.

The PA-1, MNAR addenda, earnest money rules, inspection contingencies. Minnesota's standardized forms are revised every August 1st, and the old versions retire with them. Papel works the current forms, not a national template with the state name swapped in.
